Characteristics About The Game

This game is a 2d pixelated game, with a story that is inspired from real life incidents, this game has a lot of subjects ranging from beating up, to more mature subjects such as massacres.
The video game is reccomended for those that are not faint of heart.
The game will have a timeline of 90 days, in which each day will last about 24 min. The game will be a semi-rpg-storyteller game, where it will focus on more of the story teller aspects.
